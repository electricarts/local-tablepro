# Privacy

Local TablePro runs entirely on the user's Mac. It has no analytics, telemetry, advertising, account system, or network service.

To open a database, the add-on passes the selected Local site's database host, temporary loopback port, database name, username, and password to the locally installed TablePro application in a connection URL. The add-on does not log or persist these values. A loopback-only bridge forwards the connection to Local's MySQL Unix socket and is closed when the site stops or Local exits.

TablePro and Local have their own privacy practices, which are outside this add-on's control.
